## 1. Sampling Methods (County School Board)
*Scenario: 45 schools, feedback from teachers.*

- **a. Cluster Sampling:** Randomly select 9 zip codes and interview all teachers in those schools.
- **b. Convenience Sampling:** Secretary stops teachers as they leave for lunch.
- **c. Systematic Sampling:** List schools alphabetically and select every 5th school.
- **d. Simple Random Sampling:** List all teachers, assign numbers, and select 100 randomly.
- **e. Stratified Sampling:** Separate teachers by gender and select 20% from each category.

## 3. Probability (Christine's Move)
*Scenario: Dave and Mike helping move. Probability of no-show: Dave 36%, Mike 26%.*

- **a. Both show up:** 0.64 * 0.74 = **0.47**
- **b. At least one shows up:** 1 - (0.36 * 0.26) = **0.91**
- **c. Neither shows up:** 0.36 * 0.26 = **0.09**
